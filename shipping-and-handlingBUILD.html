Order Handling & Delivery Time Estimates


  • Orders are shipped with FedEx, R+L Carriers, or USPS from Grand Rapids, MI or Phoenix, AZ.
  • Handling time for unpainted parts is 1-2 business days. Delivery time is approximately 2-7 days.
  • Handling time for painted parts is 5 business days. Delivery time is approximately 2-7 business days after the order leaves our facility.
  • Most unpainted and folded bumpers ship for free.
  • Economy plastic bumpers are FOLDED
  • Premium plastic bumpers are NOT FOLDED
  • Most small items such as Ready To Spray Paint ship for free via USPS
  • Most steel bumpers ship for free.
  • Tailgates ship for free

The available shipping options are Economy & Premium. These are defined solely by the price, the way it is packaged, and which carrier we use. Most folded plastic bumpers ship for free.


Delivery dates & timeframes are estimates made by the shipping carrier. National emergencies, weather events, and unforseen circumstances across the country can affect delivery times on rare occasions. Taxes are automatically collected for cities, counties, and states that mandate online sales tax. Shipping cost are subject to change at any time at our own discretion.
