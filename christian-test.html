What shipping company do you use?
We use multiple shipping carriers. We choose the best carrier for your specific part, because of the size of our items there are NO expedited shipping options available.

What is the difference between Economy & Premium shipping?
Economy shipping is for plastic parts and those are folded for the most economical shipping possible. Premium plastic parts will NOT be folded.

How can you ship a bumper cover folded?
We start by folding the wings of the bumper inward then we wrap the bumper in a film wrap. You may see light stress marks on the area where it was folded. If you would like an UNFOLDED part, please select the PREMIUM option.

What is your handling time?

What kind of paint do you use?
We use one of the worlds leading OEM automotive coatings on everything that we paint.

What is the painting process?
The paint job is of the highest quality; we follow OEM specs for painting. We paint all our parts in our new state-of-the-art paint booth. We apply the 3 coats of base color and then they are coated with 2 coats of clear coat, before it goes into the baking tunnel. The parts then pass through our quality control inspection before they are shipped out of our facilities.

How can I be sure the part I purchase will fit my vehicle?
Our one of a kind fitment compatibility questionnaire qualifies each purchase. Please use the drop-down questions on the homepage to verify the item fits your vehicle. Please make sure the picture that you see is what you need. Unfortunately, we cannot decode VIN numbers in order to verify fitment.

How long should I wait to power wash/wax my car?
Allow 2 weeks after you receive your item for the paint to fully cure, the more sunshine the better!
